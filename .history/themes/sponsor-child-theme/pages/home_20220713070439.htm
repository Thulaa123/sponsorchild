When you sponsor a child you can change the life of a child in Sri Lanka for good, for less than Â£1 a day.

When you sponsor a child with us, you will be doing so much more than just helping them. You will help to transform the lives of their family and wider community in Sri Lanka.

Your Sponsored Child

You'll help improve the life of your sponsored child and other vulnerable children in the community through access to nutrition, healthcare, education, clean water and a safe environment.

Sponsoring a child helps create the teachers, nurses and community leaders of the future.

The Child's Family

Your sponsorship will make sure families have the means to provide for their children. We help families gain a stable income so they can afford all of life's essentials, like healthcare, nutrition and education.
